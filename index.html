Medicine

Krok-1

Krok-1 is a Licensing Exam, which is taken after the students complete the courses in basic fundamental sciences that are covered in the Krok-1 Test-Based Exam. It is held during the 3rd year of study.

Medicine

Krok-2

Krok-2 is a Licensing exam on profession-specific subjects covered in the curriculum for qualification of a Specialist. Krok-2 is a part of graduation examination and is held during the last year of study.
