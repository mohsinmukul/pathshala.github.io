About Us

Pathshala

Pathshala is an online learning destination that provides a premier online learning experience that honors each students individual learning style and educational needs. Pathshala offers a challenging course of study for students in collage, with college prep academics, and Honors opportunities. Our philosophy of education respects each child’s gifts, talents, and learning style. At Pathshala, students enjoy personalized, one-to-one teaching and learning that is not often found in a home school setting.

Pathshala offers quality college prep, honors academic programs. Our interactive online and common core aligned text-based courses inspire critical thinking, and are supported by caring teachers who will build one-on-one relationships with each child for a positive learning experience.

Pathshala believes in and encourages lifelong learning and the enthusiasm for fulfilling lifetime dreams through educational opportunity for all students. We are totally committed to your learning needs and can provide assistance to special needs students.

Mission

Pathshala is committed to providing interactive, media-rich, college preparatory, and educational opportunities for collage in a flexible, student-centered learning environment to a diverse population of learners.

Pathshala does not discriminate in employment or the provision of educational services on the basis of race, color, religion, age, gender, national origin, disability, citizenship status, veteran status or any other characteristic protected by federal, state or local law.
